Clinical Innovation & Technology Projects
📊
RG-RST — RAGT Readiness Scoring Tool (V2)
Web-based 8-domain clinical decision support system for EksoNR, HIWIN, and R-GAIT patient selection. Adopted by 5 PTs and 20+ interns at Thai Red Cross. Designed for future ML integration and multi-center use.
→ rehabarchitect.github.io/RAGT-Scoring-Tool-V2
🦿
DKES — Dynamic Knee Control Elastic Sling
Original 2-joint elastic bandaging technique for concurrent knee hyperextension and foot drop in neurological patients. Biomechanical rationale: ankle neutral bias, tibia mobility, slight knee flexion → improved stance stability. No identical technique identified in current literature.
💨
Breathe Belt — Respiratory Rehabilitation Device
Co-developed with SUT School of Engineering. Targeted at intubated patients for airway clearance and respiratory muscle training. Pilot study (n=20, post-abdominal surgery) presented at HACC 2022. Funded by TED Fund (Youth Startup Program) — 100,000 THB grant.
⚡
Breaking the Wall of Walking — Falling Walls Lab Thailand Finalist
Proposed a scalable home-based rehabilitation model for Thailand's aging society, presented at Falling Walls Lab Thailand (lab23TH). National access framework for post-stroke community rehabilitation.
